About Us

Divine Himalayan Yatra is a dedicated spiritual travel company with over 18 years of experience in organizing sacred journeys across Uttarakhand. We believe in curating not just tours, but transformative pilgrimages guided by compassion, safety, and devotion. Our team includes local guides well-versed in Himalayan traditions, providing seamless experiences with a deep respect for spirituality and cultural heritage.
Every year, hundreds of devotees entrust us with their Char Dham, Do Dham, and single temple yatras, joining a growing family on the path of faith. Walk the ancient trails, meditate by holy rivers, and rediscover yourself.
